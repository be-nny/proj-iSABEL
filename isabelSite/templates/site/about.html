How To Play

  • > Scan the barcode of an item that you have bought
  • > Find a bin
  • > Scan the QR code on the bin to redeem your points
  • > Repeat to level up and buy rewards with your XP

After much planning and deliberation, iSABEL imagined a system where users could scan the barcodes of food items purchased on campus and receive XP rewards based on their sustainability ranking. Scanning an item would add it to a user's virtual 'basket'. Items that are vegetarian, vegan, don't contain palm oil, have recyclable packaging etc., encourage faster levelling. Players compete to top the leaderboard and help to reduce littering, promote recycling, and more sustainable food options on campus.
